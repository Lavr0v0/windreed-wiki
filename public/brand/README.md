## Mark logic

- Six reeds represent the six formal party members.
- The three wind currents stand for meeting, travelling, and choosing to remain together.
- The narrowing path carries the “Wayfarers” half of the name.
- The compass star indicates direction without implying a fixed leader.
- Reeds bend in the same wind while keeping different heights and silhouettes.

For professional print, convert the editable text in the horizontal lockup to outlines in the layout application. The circular crest is already font-independent.
